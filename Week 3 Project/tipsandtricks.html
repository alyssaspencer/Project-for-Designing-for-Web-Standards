  • Carving 360
  • Vertival Backhand Snap
  • Backside air Reverse
  • Going Vertical
  • Backside Tube Riding
  • The Rodeo Flip
And many more

Tips

Most surfers have their own style of riding the waves. Fortunately, lots of the pro surfers of the world have given us their advice to share with you.

The first tip is an Indo Board. This board has been used for surf-like exercise(s) when you can't be out on the water. Thses exercises include:

  • Push Ups
  • Squats
  • The Cross Step
  • Nose Riding
  • Reverse
Most have concluded that this mini type board is amazing for indoor training and such.
